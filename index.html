Welcome

Welcome to Jacobs Property. A Northampton based property company specialising in the letting, management and development of properties. A company which conducts itself professionally with great care for customer service. We pride ourselves on our properties and the manner in which we do business and treat our clients. The owner and operator of Jacobs Property is Jonathan Jacob Curtis. He grew up in Northampton attending local schools and has forged many ties within the local community through living and working here for over 30 years.

Jon joined the property ladder when he was 18 and has been developing and growing his portfolio ever since. Jon is a fully certified landlord accredited by local and National institutes like the National Landlords Association. Jacobs Property owns and manages a range of properties including individual households and HMO's (House in Multiple Occupancy).

Jon is also a fully certified Heating Engineer & Plumber. He is a Gas Safe registered Engineer who is experienced and time served to the highest standards of domestic qualification with currently over 12 years spent working with British Gas. He excels in fault resolution, maintenance and installation with further qualifications in efficiency, renewable energy and varying fuel types.
